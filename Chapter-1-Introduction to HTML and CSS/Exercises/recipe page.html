Classic Alfredo Pasta

A creamy and indulgent Italian pasta dish that's perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Method

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the fettuccine pasta and cook according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Slowly pour in the heavy cream, stirring constantly. Bring to a simmer.
  4. Gradually whisk in the grated Parmesan cheese until the sauce is smooth and creamy.
  5. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Add the cooked fettuccine to the skillet and toss until well coated in the sauce.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with chopped fresh parsley.
